Taking Speech and Language Therapy to the Next Level

At Next Level Therapy, our goal is simple: Help your child achieve new levels with clinical based evaluations, analysis, treatment plans, and one-to-one bilingual and monolingual speech therapy services tailored specifically to his or her needs.

Your child is our specific focus, but you and your entire family benefit from our family-oriented services. You get a detailed treatment plan that not only helps your child reach his or her best, but also supports your entire family with the tools needed to succeed in therapy.

Why choose Next Level Therapy?

Clinical Approach with School Curriculum Understanding

You get the best of both worlds. Your child works closely with a therapist who has a deep understanding of what your school’s curriculum requires from children’s communication and language skills and extensive knowledge of clinical therapy techniques. This means your child’s treatment plan focuses on what is expected from them in a school environment. Paired with clinically proven techniques that are tried and true, your child’s speech and language skills will improve in his or her school setting and at home.

Bilingual Therapy

If your child is bilingual, you know it is important to provide culturally and linguistically appropriate therapy and materials in languages besides English. NLT is one of the few speech therapy programs in New York that offers Bilingual therapy in Spanish and is working to expand into other languages.

Customized Treatment Plan

Your child needs a different approach when it comes to speech and language therapy. You’ll get an evaluation report based on a detailed clinical analysis of where your child is right now with speech and language. After a thorough evaluation and analysis, your son or daughter is assigned a specific treatment plan customized for his or her particular impairment and speech and language goals.

Constant Knowledge Base Expansion

Rest assured, you and your child get a professional, clinical approach to treatment that applies evidence based techniques and strategies in your child’s therapy. Your child’s therapist is dedicated to improving therapy methods by staying current and up-to-date on the latest research and developments in speech pathology. You never need to worry that your child will face fads or fly-by-night treatments. Both you and your child will benefit from your therapist’s dedication to extending and building on accepted knowledge in the field.

Continued Post-Session and Supplemental Therapy education

Research has shown that carryover is an effective strategy in achieving success in therapy. This means you get a family-oriented approach that equips your entire family with knowledge and techniques to continue supplemental therapy outside of session hours.

Convenient Sessions

You don’t come to us, we come to you. Therapy services are provided in the comfortable setting of your own home. Schedules are agreed upon in advance between you and your child’s therapist, with a heavy emphasis on your child’s availability. The least disruption to you and your child, the better.
